NEC 705

Covers power source that is operating in parallel (interconnected) with grid typically (primary source)

*-With supply side connection, disconnect must be on service side of fuses

*-Supply side connections rated 1,000A< must have ground fault protection

*-For load side connections, each source interconnection must have dedicated circuit breaker or fusible disconnect means

-Permanent plaque or directory describing power sources on premises

-Installation requirement vary depending on point of connection (POC)
